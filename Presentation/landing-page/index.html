The Last

Stand

Description

In future resources and minerals of Earth were exhausted, and humanity moved their mining on the another planets. This is the responsibility of the corporation Red Stone Corporation (R.S.C.). For this purpose was created spaceships with the Space Troopers, aim of which is reconnaissance operations on the new planets to their colonization.
The plot of the game was set in 2513, after the crash of one of these spaceships on the planet Illium, mysteriously opened the portal to Earth. All members of crew during the crash is died. Only one survived trooper decided by all means complete the mission. But in the bowels of Illium his found thing what he didn’t expect… This trooper, is the last stand between these creatures and the Earth.
